ABOUT

5 SECONDS OF SUMMER, OFTEN SHORTENED TO 5SOS (PRONOUNCED "FIVE SAUCE"), ARE AN AUSTRALIAN POP ROCK BAND FORMED IN SYDNEY IN 2011. THE GROUP CONSISTS OF LEAD VOCALIST AND RHYTHM GUITARIST LUKE HEMMINGS, LEAD GUITARIST MICHAEL CLIFFORD, BASSIST CALUM HOOD, AND DRUMMER ASHTON IRWIN.
